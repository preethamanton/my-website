About Me

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented Bachelor of Pharmacy student at PSG College of Pharmacy expected to graduate in 2025. During my tenure as a Summer Research Fellow at the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, I developed robust technical and molecular skills in instrumentation such as RT PCR and Western Blot. My research acumen is evidenced by multiple publications and presentations at national conferences where I have earned awards for my work on AI in life sciences and environmental health. As a proactive member of academic committees and clubs, I have honed strong analytical, regulatory, and quality control skills along with effective communication and project planning abilities. I am continually enhancing my expertise through ongoing education in drug regulatory affairs, intellectual property rights, and machine learning. My aim is to contribute significantly to pharmaceutical sciences and innovation.
